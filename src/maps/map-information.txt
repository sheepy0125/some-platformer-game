Map information!
A map must meet these requirements:
    * Must have rows and columns of tiles
    * Each tile must be a number from 0 to 9
    * Cannot contain lines with newlines

Tile dictionary:
    0 = sky
    1 = dirt
    2 = (unused)
    3 = (unused)
    4 = (unused)
    5 = (unused)
    6 = (unused)
    7 = (unused)
    8 = (unused)
    9 = player tile

Unused tiles will appear as 
`/src/assets/images/tiles/error.png`
